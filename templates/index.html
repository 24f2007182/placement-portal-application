About Us

The Campus Placement Portal is designed to streamline the recruitment process between students and companies. It provides a centralized platform where students can create professional profiles, upload resumes, and apply for placement drives organized by companies.

Companies can post job opportunities, review student applications, and shortlist candidates for interviews. The portal ensures a transparent and efficient placement process managed by the administrator.

Best Practices

For Students

  • Keep your profile information updated.
  • Upload a clear and professional resume.
  • Apply only to positions that match your skills.
  • Regularly check application status and notifications.

For Companies

  • Provide clear job descriptions and requirements.
  • Review applications carefully before shortlisting.
  • Update application statuses to keep students informed.

For Administrators

  • Approve genuine companies before allowing job postings.
  • Monitor placement drives for transparency.
  • Ensure smooth communication between students and recruiters.
