About the Portal

The Placement Portal simplifies campus recruitment by providing a unified system where students apply for opportunities, companies conduct hiring drives, and administrators manage the process.

The system ensures transparent application tracking, efficient recruitment workflows, and improved communication between students and recruiters.

Why Use This Portal

  • Centralized placement drive management
  • Easy application tracking
  • Transparent recruitment workflow
  • Efficient student-company interaction

Platform Features

Student Profiles

Students create detailed profiles including skills, experience, and resumes to apply for placement drives.

Company Recruitment

Companies can post job openings, create recruitment drives, and manage student applications easily.

Admin Control

Administrators oversee the entire placement process, approve companies, and monitor applications.

Best Practices for Students

  • Maintain an updated resume and skill profile.
  • Apply early to placement drives.
  • Prepare thoroughly for interviews.
  • Track your application progress regularly.
  • Improve technical and communication skills.
  • Participate in mock interviews and workshops.
